		          1         2         3         4         5        
		01234567890123456789012345678901234567890123456789012345678
		DABCEFG09

		Start	End	Field Description
		0	0	Player location
		1	1	Item 1 Location (Earth Elemental Statue 
				(index 0))
		2	2	Item 2 Location (Wind Elemental Statue 
				(index 1))
		3	3	Item 3 Location (Fire Elemental Statue 
				(index 2))
		4	4	Item 4 Location (Water Elemental Statue 
				(index 3))
		5	5	Item 5 Location (Wood Elemental Statue 
				(index 4))
		6	6	Item 6 Location (Metal Elemental Statue 
				(index 5))
		7	7	Lock Status (1 = Locked, 0 = Unlocked)
		8	8	Current Item (Values 0-5 represent their 
				corresponding item index, 9 is default - 
				no item current)
